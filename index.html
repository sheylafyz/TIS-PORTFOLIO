VIDEO VISIT UTM DIGITAL

On December 5th, I joined a Webex session by UTM Digital, skillfully moderated by Dr. Aryati, which lasted two enlightening hours. The talk gave a comprehensive overview of working within UTM Digital, touching on digital services, infrastructure, networking, ICT security management, and system development. The adoption of the scrum method for system development illustrated UTM Digital's innovative approach to work and decision-making. Overall, this informative session not only broadened my knowledge but also impressed me with the scope of UTM Digital's efforts and the tangible benefits they provide.

PC ASSEMBLE

PC assembling is our lab activity, where we have to disassemble and then reassemble or put back together the components as they were originally. The challenge that can be taken is remembering the steps to reassemble the compartments back to their original state. In addition, we can also recognize the tools by looking directly and knowing their uses. This experience is very useful for us to know the uses of PC tools and also to identify possible errors that may occur if something happens in the future.
